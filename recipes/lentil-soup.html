Lentil soup

Description

Need comfort during dark, winter days? Want a rich, tasty soup after a long day of work? Look no further! This lentil soup is as delicious as it is nourishing. It's the best darn thing you can imagine!

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • A large onion, diced
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 6 large, fresh tomatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • A stick of celery, diced

Steps

  1. Dunk the tomatoes in a pot of boiling water and rinse them under a cold tap. The skin should now peel off easily
  2. Saut√©e the onion, celery and carrot
  3. Chop the garlic and add to the onion, fry for about thirty seconds
  4. Add the tomatoes, vegetable stock and lentils. Add plenty of water
  5. Top off with cheese
