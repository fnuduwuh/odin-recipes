Rice and beans

Description

Rice and beans has been a staple meal for lots of people. It can be for you too! Rich in starches and in protein, itś a great meal thatś also suitable for vegetarians.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black eyed beans
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 6 large, fresh tomatoes, diced
  • A large onion, diced
  • Two bell peppers, diced
  • Two carrots, diced
  • Smoked paprika powder
  • 16 jalapeños

Steps

  1. Sautée the onion, add the carrot and bell peppers
  2. Chop the garlic and add to the onion, fry for about thirty seconds
  3. Add the tomatoes, jalapeños and smoked paprika powder and simmer on low heat
  4. After 20 minutes, add the beans and heat them for a bit
  5. Top off with cheese
